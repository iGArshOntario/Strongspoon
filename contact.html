❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Right now we are Regina, SK only. We prepare every cup fresh for you, so local delivery is essential to the quality. We're working on expanding — stay tuned!
Most flavours contain 46g+ of protein per 250g cup before any toppings are added. The Power Mix (peanut butter) is ~30g+ since it uses peanut butter instead of a protein blend. Adding toppings like nuts will naturally increase the protein content further.
Our base is made with a cultured dairy base, so it is not dairy-free. For gluten: the base itself is gluten-free, but some toppings (like certain nuts processed in shared facilities) may carry cross-contamination risk. If you have a serious allergy, please email us before ordering.
No sugar added — that's one of our core commitments. All sweetness comes naturally from the ingredients. If you add fruit toppings like Apple or Blueberries, they contain natural fruit sugars.
We require a minimum of 12 hours advance notice since every cup is made fresh to order. You can schedule delivery up to several days ahead — just pick your preferred date and time slot at checkout.
Delivery is $4.99 for orders under $25. Orders of $25 or more get free delivery. Pickup is always free — you can select "Pickup" at checkout.
Since every order is prepared fresh, cancellations must be requested at least 6 hours before your scheduled delivery time. Please email us at Strongspoon.ca@gmail.com as soon as possible and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
